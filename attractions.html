Places to Visit

  • East Jersey Old Town Village

    Description: Step back in time at East Jersey Old Town Village, a living history museum that recreates life in the 18th century. Explore historic buildings, interact with costumed interpreters, and experience the daily life of early European settlers in New Jersey.

  • Rutgers Gardens

    Description: Explore the beauty of Rutgers Gardens, a 180-acre botanical garden with diverse plant collections, scenic trails, and educational programs. Enjoy the tranquility of the bamboo forest, admire the colorful flower displays, and learn about sustainable gardening practices.
