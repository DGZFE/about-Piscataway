Historical Timeline

  • 1666

    Piscataway is founded by European settlers, including a group of English colonists from New Haven and Milford, Connecticut, and a group of Dutch and French Huguenot settlers from Staten Island.

  • 1683

    The first Raritan Landing, a major port and trading center, is established on the Raritan River in Piscataway.

  • 1701

    The first church in Piscataway, St. James Church, is built.

  • 1777

    George Washington and the Continental Army pass through Piscataway during the Revolutionary War. The area witnesses skirmishes and troop movements.

  • 1860

    The Raritan and Delaware Bay Railroad opens a line through Piscataway, connecting the area to New York City and Philadelphia.

  • 1917

    Camp Kilmer, a major military training camp, is established in Piscataway during World War I. It later serves as a processing center for returning soldiers.

  • 1950s

    Piscataway experiences significant suburban growth after World War II, with the development of new housing and commercial areas.

  • 1963

    Rutgers University establishes a campus in Piscataway, which becomes the university's main campus.

  • 1990s

    Piscataway continues to diversify, with a growing population of immigrants from Asia, Latin America, and other parts of the world.

  • Present

    Piscataway is a vibrant and diverse township, home to a mix of residential, commercial, and industrial areas. It continues to evolve and adapt to the changing needs of its residents.
